📖 How to Play

  1. Player 1 always starts the game first.
  2. Click on any empty square to place your symbol.
  3. First player to align the required number of symbols wins!
  4. Customize grid size and win conditions in the settings above.
  5. Use keyboard arrows to navigate and Enter/Space to select.
